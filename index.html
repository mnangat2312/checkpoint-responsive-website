Menu

Chocolate Coloured Ugali

Ksh 250
This is ugali made with wheat flour, cassava and millet. It will leave you wanting more.
Best served with suchaa and the Pokot yoghurt.

Pokot Ash Yoghurt

Ksh. 150
Known in local dialect as mala ya kienyeji, it's made from the processing of goat's milk.The yoghurt is a product with intense flavor and high nutritional value. The ash comes from the wood of cromwo tree. It provides disinfectant properties and a unique aromatic taste.

Traditional Goat Meat

Ksh. 600
The goat fry is essentially very well seasoned and perfectly balanced goat meat that is slow cooked and simmered till it is almost dry. It is absolutely filling, delicious and inviting.
Served with ugali, sukuma wiki and kachumbari.

Roasted Goat Leg

Ksh. 1000
Goat meat is marinated in a flavourful herb and spice marinade, and then slowly grilled, to give you some tender, and delicious meat that you will certainly love!!
